Who We Are

WISEFAM is a Ugandan-based enterprise committed to delivering a diverse range of services that promote women's empowerment, environmental sustainability, community well-being, and quality education. Established in 2020, the company offers women's empowerment services and cleaning services for residential, commercial, and institutional spaces. Additionally, it provides consultancy services in environmental conservation, guiding individuals, businesses, and communities toward sustainable practices that preserve natural resources and mitigate climate change effects. With a strong emphasis on health and nutrition, the company also educates communities on proper dietary practices, food security, and sustainable agriculture, fostering self-reliance and improved livelihoods.

Beyond environmental and nutritional initiatives, WISEFAM plays a significant role in education and humanitarian support. It conducts primary school training programs, equipping young learners with essential knowledge in environmental conservation, nutrition, and sustainable farming. Recognizing the urgent needs of displaced populations, the company is also actively involved in refugee rescue services, providing emergency support, guidance, and sustainable solutions for refugees to rebuild their lives. With a dedicated team of experts and a mission-driven approach, WISEFAM continues to impact communities across Uganda by fostering a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable future.
